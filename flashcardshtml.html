10th Grade Geometry Review

What is the sum of interior angles in a triangle?
180 degrees
Define a perpendicular bisector.
A line that divides a segment into two equal parts at 90°
What is the Pythagorean Theorem?
a² + b² = c² for right triangles
What is a tangent to a circle?
A line that touches the circle at exactly one point
What is the formula for the area of a triangle?
½ × base × height
What is a central angle?
An angle whose vertex is the center of a circle
What is a parallelogram?
A quadrilateral with opposite sides parallel and equal
What is the distance formula?
√[(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²]
